SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS

Find out about the minimum specs and recommended system requirements you'll need
to play ANTRAVITY 2 on your favourite platform

MINIMUM SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS [PC]

• OS: Windows 7 64bit or Linux
• Processor: AMD Athlon X2 or Intel Core 2 Duo
• Memory: 2 GB
• Graphics Card: AMD Radeon HD 4200 or equivalent 
• Direct X: 10
• Drive Space: 1 GB

RECOMMENDED SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS [PC]

• OS: Windows 10 64bit or Linux
• Processor: AMD Phenom X4 or Intel Core 2 quad
• Memory: 4 GB
• Graphics Card: Nvidia GT710 or equivalent 
• Direct X: 11
• Drive Space: 1.5 GB

SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS [ANDROID]

• OS: Android 4.4 or later
• Memory: 1 GB
• Drive Space: 200 MB
